<h2 class="wp-block-heading">The final statement</h2>



<p><strong><strong><strong><strong>‘There is little hope for the fairness of the trial’.</strong></strong></strong></strong></p>



<p>I will be brief. I was defending my homeland, I was serving my Fatherland. I hope for a fair trial, but taking into account everything I have experienced since I was captured, the absurd accusations of me seizing power, terrorism, then there is little hope for the fairness of the trial. And only justifying me in this situation can be fair and legitimate.</p>



<p>Southern District Military Court, Rostov-on-Don, Russia</p>



<p>5 March 2025</p>
