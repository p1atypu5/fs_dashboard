<h2 class="wp-block-heading">The final statement</h2>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>‘I demand the maximum sentence. Because I despise you. I will not live on  this land or in this country with you.’</strong></p>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">Your Honor, everything the prosecution says I wrote online is true. I did everything correctly, I plead not guilty, and I do not repent. I publicly justify, ardently approve of, and support the bombing of the Crimean Bridge in the autumn of 2022 and the attacks by Ukrainian drones on the Kremlin and other targets in Moscow and the Moscow region in May 2023. Because Ukraine is a victim of aggression from Russia, and it has the right to retaliate against the military facilities of the aggressor country. And for myself, I demand the maximum sentence. Because I despise you. I will not share this land or state with you. Since I cannot leave for Ukraine now, lock me up in prison forever. Victory will still be with those who are destroying Putin’s bastards with weapons. Glory to Ukraine! Putin is… (I will say this openly in court).</p>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">Konakovsky City Court, Tver Region, Russia</p>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">24 July 2023</p>
