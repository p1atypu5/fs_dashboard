<h2 class="wp-block-heading">The Final Statement</h2>



<h3 class="wp-block-heading">&#8216;As I stated earlier in my address to the court, I believe we are being convicted unlawfully.&#8217;</h3>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph"></p>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">Your Honour, I had prepared a final statement, but today, as I was being escorted out of my cell, my statement and a motion I had prepared were confiscated without any explanation. So I will simply say this: I do not admit this charge in its entirety. As I stated earlier in my address to the court, I believe we are being prosecuted unlawfully. We had no involvement in the alleged offence. I have nothing further to add. I am not guilty.</p>
